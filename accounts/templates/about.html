Products and Services

Email ID Scanning: Our tool scans email addresses for potential phishing attempts and fraudulent content, helping users identify and avoid malicious emails before they can cause harm.

URL Scanner: This feature evaluates URLs for safety, detecting harmful links and alerting users to potential threats, ensuring safer browsing experiences.

Malware Scanner: Our robust malware scanning capabilities identify malicious software from devices, protecting user data and maintaining system integrity.

Network Scanner: This service assesses the security of entire networks, detecting vulnerabilities and providing insights to enhance overall cybersecurity.
